Why Mama is the Greatest Mother in All of History

Mama is the greatest mom of all time for many different reasons. First, she is very forgiving when you don't help her and she has to do everything. Also, she makes wonderful food and she takes me to different places. We make her very, very busy, but she still loves us in the end. We owe Mama everything and she is the best person in the world.
